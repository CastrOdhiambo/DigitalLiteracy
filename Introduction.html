Introduction

Computers are everywhere in today’s world. From smartphones to desktops, they help us work, learn, and connect. This chapter introduces you to the basics of using a computer, building confidence and familiarity with its parts and functions.

Parts of a Computer

  • Monitor: Displays information visually.
  • Keyboard: Used to type text and commands.
  • Mouse/Touchpad: Helps you navigate and interact with items on the screen.
  • CPU (Central Processing Unit): The “brain” of the computer where processing happens.
  • Storage: Saves files, documents, and applications.

Basic Skills

To get comfortable with a computer, practice these skills:

  • Turning the computer on and off properly.
  • Using the mouse to click, double-click, and drag items.
  • Typing on the keyboard and using special keys like Enter, Shift, and Backspace.
  • Opening and closing applications.
  • Saving and retrieving files.
