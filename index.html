About This Course

Built for Real People,
in a Real Kenyan Context

This is not a generic computer course. Every lesson is designed around the real situations Kenyans face every day — from opening a computer for the first time, to paying taxes on KRA iTax, to writing a professional CV and sending it by email.

Tips for Success

Go at Your Own Pace

There is no deadline. Spend as much time as you need on each chapter before moving to the next.

Practice Every Step on a Real Computer

Don't just read — open the computer and do it. Practical repetition builds real confidence fast.

Print the Checklists

Tick off each skill as you learn it. Seeing your progress on paper motivates you to keep going.

Teach Someone Else

The best way to truly master a skill is to teach it. Help a friend or family member — both of you benefit.

Stay Safe Online Always

Never share your password, National ID, or M-Pesa PIN — not even to someone claiming to be from the government or your bank.

Chapter 7 Highlight

Kenya's Essential Government Portals

Chapter 7 walks you through each portal step by step — with exact instructions so you never get lost, and never have to pay someone else to do what you can do yourself.

🏛️

eCitizen

Passport, birth certificate, business registration, and 100+ services in one portal.

🧾

KRA iTax

File tax returns, get your KRA PIN, and pay taxes online — avoid penalties.

🎓

HELB

Apply for student loans, check your balance, and make loan repayments online.

📚

KUCCPS

University and college placement after KCSE — apply and track your selection online.

🚗

NTSA

Check your driving licence, vehicle status, and road tax — all online.

🏥

SHA / NHIF

Check your Social Health Authority contributions and health insurance status.

📱

M-Pesa PayBill

Pay KPLC, Safaricom Home, Nairobi Water, school fees and more via M-Pesa PayBill.

🌐

Huduma Centre

Book appointments and access government services online without queuing.
