How It Works

Tick the chapters you want — or select all for the complete book. Each card shows estimated pages and content coverage.

Set your name, class, and the book year. Choose whether to include chapter divider pages and the cover page.

Staple, spiral bind, or use a binder. Print double-sided for a compact book. Add a laminated cover for durability.
